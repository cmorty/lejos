What next?

The previous topics have been the more basic parts of the leJOS platform.
There exist further packages, though:
  • josx.platform.rcx.remotecontrol for usage of the LEGO® remote control
  • josx.rcxcomm deals with communications
  • josx.robotics contains classes for the behavior pattern and for navigation
  • josx.vision adds vision abilities to your robot
However, those ones are concerned with more specialized aspects of roboting and hence are dealt with in the specialized trails of this tutorial.
